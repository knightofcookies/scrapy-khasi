U Teruaki "Jimmy" Murakami, (村上輝明　Murakami Teruaki, 5 Iaiong 1933 – 16 February 2014) u dei u nongshna jingrwai bad nongpyniaid ba na Japan America.
La pawnam ia ka na ka bynta ki bynta jong ka ha ka phlim Nightmare Alley, Kiss of Death, Red River, bad ha ka phlim The Killing.
Ki briew Tupí ki shong ha jan baroh ki rud duriaw jong ka Brazil ha ka por ba ki nong Portugal ki la poi nyngkong eh hangta.
U dei ruh u nongseng ia ka Leading University ha Sylhet, Bangladesh.
