Ka jingïakynduh (2001-2007)
Ka jingbym don carbon bad ka jingbym don nuclear (2007)
Ki jingpyniap briew na ka bynta ka burom ki iadei bad ka thain Mihngi Pdeng bad ka Asia Sepngi.
Kumba long ka para marjan ba jan jong ka, ka New Guinea, ka Australia ka la long shisien ka bynta jong ka dewbah ba shathie jong ka Gondwanaland.
Ha u snem 2012 kane ka klob ka la ïoh rung sha ka kyrdan ïalehkai ba ha khlieh duh jong ki shynrang jong ka Sweden.
Ka Asian Handball Federation (AHF) () ka dei ka kynhun kaba pyniaid ia ka jingialehkai kti bad ka jingialehkai beach handball ha Asia.
