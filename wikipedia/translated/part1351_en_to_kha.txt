U Gjergj Xhuvani (20 December 1963 – 14 August 2019) u dei u nongpyniaid phlim, nongthoh phlim, bad nongpynmih phlim na Albania.
Ka string baroh kawei mynta ka long kumne: "f r".
Ki dkhot jong kane ka "dorbar" ki long ki ri satellite, kiba laitluid ha ka rukom kaba pura, hynrei la synshar beit sha ka lynti kaba dei da ka Soviet Union ryngkat bad ki shipai, lada ki shim ba ka long kaba donkam.
